Welcome to the Hackathon Crowdfunding System

This platform is designed to help hackathon organizers and participants raise funds for their innovative projects.

How it works

  1. Organizers create a project page and set a fundraising goal.
  2. Participants can browse and support projects by making a donation.
  3. Once a project reaches its fundraising goal, the funds are released to the organizer.
